Gutenberg-University Mainz

Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Project DiCi-Hub (Apr. 2021 - Aug. 2024)

Details

Research Hub for Cinema Studies (DiCi-Hub). In this project DiCihub I have worked as a postdoctoral researcher supervising the collection, digitization, and data structuration of Super8 film catalogues from the 70s and early 80s. I was also the project coordinator between 2022 and 2023. As a scientist at Dicihub, I worked in an interdisciplinary team, organized workshops and public lectures, co-organized international conferences, and contributed to the public dissemination of the project's aims and findings.
